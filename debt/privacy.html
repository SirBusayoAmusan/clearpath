Privacy policy

Last updated:

This privacy policy explains how Clearpath ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you visit clearpath.lifestyle (the "site"). We've tried to write it in plain language. If anything is unclear, email us at info@clearpath.lifestyle.

The short version

The numbers you type into our debt tracker never leave your device — we don't receive them, store them, or sell them. The main data collected on this site comes from standard third-party services like advertising and analytics, described below. We don't ask you to create an account.

Information the tools do not collect

Our debt snowball tracker and any similar calculators on this site run entirely in your browser. The debt balances, interest rates, and payment amounts you enter are processed locally on your own device to produce your results. This information is never transmitted to us or any server. When you close or refresh the page, it is gone unless you have printed it yourself.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, we and our third-party partners may automatically collect certain technical information when you visit, such as your approximate location (country/region), browser type, device type, the pages you view, and how you arrived at the site. This is used to understand how the site is used and to keep it running well.

Cookies and advertising

We use cookies and similar technologies to run the site and to display ads that help keep our tools free. Specifically:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

Third-party ad networks and analytics providers may set their own cookies, over which we have no direct control. Most web browsers let you refuse or delete cookies through their settings.

Analytics

We may use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to collect aggregated, anonymized data about how visitors use the site. This helps us improve our tools and content. These services may set cookies and collect information such as pages visited and time spent on the site.

Children's privacy

This site is intended for adults and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings and opt out of personalized advertising using the links above. Because our tools don't collect your personal financial data in the first place, there's nothing for us to delete on that front.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email info@clearpath.lifestyle.
